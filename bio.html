About me!

Click my profile to see a irl picture!

I am a 20-year-old artist who grew up all over. I lived in Virgina, New Jersey, Maryland, Texas, Germany, and Canada. I completed my high school degree in Texas and attended art school in Canada. I currently attend a community college in Texas.
My art varies with multiple mediums, my most preferred being digital art, but I also enjoy colored pencils, watercolor, and sewing. I love starting projects to keep myself active and busy, my most recent being a visual novel I created in Google slides.
My main influences are online mutuals and pieces of media I grew up on, mostly 90s movies and music with occasional 80s influences. I also love older technology; with my oldest piece I own being a working 1978 two-way channel radio. My biggest dream is to one day either own or repair a functional cassette player.
