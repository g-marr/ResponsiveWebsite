About me!

Click my profile to see a irl picture!

I am a 20-year-old artist who grew up all over. I lived in Virgina, New Jersey, Maryland, Texas, Germany, and Canada. Out of all the locations I stayed at, I enjoyed Canada and Germany the most. I found out I loved using public transit when living in those places, and I used the train all the time to get to places. I currently live in Texas, which is a bummer since I loved walking in Germany (not so much Canada). I graduated high school here in Texas and did some more traveling, which is where the stint in Canada came from. I attended art school there for half a year before moving back to the U.S.A to attend a local college.

My art varies with multiple mediums, my most preferred being digital art, but I also enjoy colored pencils, watercolor, and sewing. I'm currently working on making my wardrobe more unique and my current project is sewing up a denim skirt! I love starting projects to keep myself active and busy, my most recent computer project being a visual novel I created in Google slides.

My main influences are online mutuals and pieces of media I grew up on, mostly 90s movies and music with occasional 80s influences. I also love older technology; with my oldest piece I own being a working 1978 two-way channel radio and the most impressive piece I own is a working Smith Corona typewriter from the 80s. My biggest dream is to one day either own or repair a functional cassette player.
